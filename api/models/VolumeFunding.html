VolumeFunding

final case class VolumeFunding(volume: Volume, funder: Party, grant: Option[String]) extends TableRow with InVolume with Product with Serializable

A funding source associated with a Volume, where the agency is represented as a party. This interface is temporary, quick and dirty, just to provide minimal functionality, and should not be expected to remain as is.
